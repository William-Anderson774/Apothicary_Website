A note on the work

Project Apothecary — a digital reference guide to the history, materials, and practice of the apothecary tradition. Built as an archival object: something that feels found rather than made.
Course Core 2 Interactions
Parsons School of Design · Spring 2026
Professor Ji Park
Author William Anderson · 2026 · @__willanderson__
Concept The apothecary sits at the intersection of science, craft, and community — a figure who has been largely erased by the modern pharmacy. This site attempts to restore some of that texture: the herbs, the instruments, the recipes, the arc of the practice from ancient remedy to chain drugstore. The design is intentionally archival — antiquewhite, borders, Georgia type — to echo the material culture of the spaces it documents.
